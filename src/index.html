Instructions

  • Use your WASD keys to move your frog
  • The main objective is to guide your frog to any of the active Drop Zones
  • Once all drop zones have been filled, you must begin your journey again, only with more haste this time
  • Be careful of the Cars and Trucks that fill the road, they will end your journey swifty
  • Watch out for the fish, who will dissapear before you know it
  • Be mindful of the turtles and their shells, who will only help your traversal to a drop zone once.
